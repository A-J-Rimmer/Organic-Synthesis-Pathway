ALDEHYDES

Prefix: Formyl-

Suffix: -al (or -carbaldehyde on rings)



Testing for the Functional Group:

A)----Heat with Tollen's reagent, or

B)----Heat with Fehling's solution

-----POSITIVE RESULTS:

-----A: Silver mirror forms, as the diamminesilver(I) ions are reduced to solid silver.

-----B: Brick red precipitate forms, as the complexed copper(II) ions are reduced to copper(I) oxide.



Reactions:

> Aldehyde → Hydroxynitrile

> Aldehyde → Alcohol

> Aldehyde → Carboxylic Acid
